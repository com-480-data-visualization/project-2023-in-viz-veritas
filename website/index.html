The historical travelguides of the Bibliotheca Hertziana

Welcome, on this page you will get to explore a collection of old travel guides for Italy from the rare books collection of the Bibliotheca Hertziana in Rome.
The collection contains around 800 documents in several languages from the years 1550 up until 1930. They are sparsely illustrated and contain a lot of recognizable place names. All of them have been recently digitized, but only around 80 have also been transcribed using Transkribus. Tranksribus is an AI supported platform for text recognition and transcriptions. These are the books you will get to explore through the following visualisations. Enjoy!
